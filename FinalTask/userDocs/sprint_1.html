Final task ISS-2021 Bologna: Automated Car-Parking

Sprint 1

Requirement analysis

Glossary

  • Transport trolley: a squared robot of side RD able to pick up a car and transport it from a point of the map to another; the robot picks up and puts down cars from the square in front of it; while the car is on the robot, they fill the same space on the map;
  • Home: the home location of the trolley is the north-western corner of the room, facing south;
  • Parking-area: empty room containing the parking-slots, the home location and some maneuvering space for the trolley;
  • INDOOR-area: the area where cars wait for the trolley to pick them up; it is not part of the parking-area;
  • OUTDOOR-area: the area where the trolley leaves the cars to be picked up by their owners; it is not part of the parking-area;
  • Parking-slot: a squared portion of the parking-area of side RD where parked cars are stored by the trolley; there are six of them and they are identified by a SLOTNUM;
  • Fan: device able to lower the temperature of the parking-area;
  • Fixed obstacles: the parts of the parking-area where the trolley is not able to pass through.

User story

  1. As a user, I want that the robot starts from its home position, walks up to the INDOOR (in which it will have the face facing north), positions itself in front of a specific parking-slot and goes back to the home.
  2. From the home the robot comes back to the previous parking-slot, walks up to the OUTDOOR (in which it will have the face facing south) and goes back to its home position.

Problem analysis

Relevant aspects

  1. The system to be built will be a distributed system, consisting of several macro-components:
    • the transport trolley;
    • the weight sensor;
    • the thermometer;
    • the fan;
    • the outsonar;
    • our application (ParkManagerService) that sends commands to the robot in order to meet the requirements.
